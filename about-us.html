Follow the Steps

Our History

Founded in 2010, Follow the Steps began with a mission to showcase the natural beauty of Trondheim to outdoor enthusiasts. Our agency was established by a group of avid hikers who wanted to create a resource for both locals and visitors to explore the diverse and scenic trails of Trondheim. Over the years, we have grown into a dedicated team committed to providing detailed trail information, guided tours, and hiking resources to ensure that every hiking experience in Trondheim is safe, enjoyable, and memorable.

Our Values

At Follow the Steps, we believe in the importance of connecting with nature and promoting sustainable outdoor activities. Our values are rooted in our commitment to:

Environmental Stewardship:

We prioritize the preservation of natural landscapes and encourage responsible hiking practices to protect the environment.

Community Engagement:

We work closely with local communities to support and promote regional tourism, benefiting both hikers and residents.

Education and Safety:

We provide comprehensive trail information, safety tips, and educational resources to ensure that hikers are well-prepared and informed.

Inclusivity and Accessibility:

We strive to make hiking accessible to everyone, offering trails for all skill levels and promoting inclusive outdoor experiences.
