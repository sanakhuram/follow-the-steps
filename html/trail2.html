Estenstadmarka

Estenstadmarka is a well-loved recreational area located to the east of Trondheim, Norway. It boasts a diverse range of trails suitable for both easy walks and more challenging hikes, meandering through serene forests and picturesque open landscapes. The area caters to outdoor enthusiasts of all levels, offering opportunities for short, leisurely hikes as well as more extended trekking adventures. Whether you're looking for a peaceful nature walk or a vigorous outdoor workout, Estenstadmarka provides a beautiful and accessible natural escape close to the city.

Difficulty: Easy to Moderate

  • Distance: Varies (up to 10 km)
  • Estimated Time: 1 to 4 hours
  • Starting Point: SkLohove or Bekken
  • Best Time to Hike: Spring to Autumn

Highlights

  • Estenstadhytta cabin
  • Estenstadvatnet lake
  • lookout points with views of Trondheim

Trail Description

Estenstadmarka is a popular recreational area east of Trondheim, featuring a mix of easy and challenging trails through forests and open landscapes. It is ideal for both short hikes and longer treks.
