Bymarka

Bymarka is a large park and nature reserve on the west side of the city of Trondheim. It has an area of 80 square kilometers (31 sq. mi.) with more than 200 kilometers (120 mi.) of trails. Bymarka is very popular for hiking, running, biking, camping (and cross-country skiing in the winter). All trails are multi-use and care and consideration when riding the trails on a bike must be taken to avoid access issues.

Difficulty: Easy to Moderate

  • Distance: Varies (up to 20 km)
  • Estimated Time: 1 to 5 hours
  • Starting Point: Skistua or Lian
  • Best Time to Hike: Year-round

Highlights

  • Lake Lian
  • Gråkallen summit
  • Elgsethytta cabin

Trail Description

Bymarka offers numerous trails that wind through forests, past lakes, and up to scenic viewpoints. Popular trails include routes to Lake Lian, Gråkallen summit, and the Elgsethytta cabin. Hikers can enjoy a variety of landscapes and spot diverse flora and fauna along the way.
