Discover the best hiking trails in Trondheim

From serene forest paths to challenging mountain treks, whether you’re a seasoned hiker or a casual walker, Trondheim offers a diverse range of trails for all levels. Enjoy breathtaking views, rich wildlife, and the tranquility of nature in one of Norway's most beautiful regions.

Best Seasons for Hiking in Trondheim

  • Spring: Witness the blooming flowers and enjoy mild temperatures. Trails are less crowded, making it an ideal time for a peaceful hike.
  • Summer: The peak hiking season with long daylight hours. Perfect for tackling longer trails and enjoying the vibrant greenery.
  • Autumn: Experience the stunning fall foliage with cooler temperatures. A great time for photography and shorter hikes.
  • Winter: Some trails are accessible for winter hiking and offer a unique snowy landscape. Make sure to check trail conditions and be prepared for cold weather.
